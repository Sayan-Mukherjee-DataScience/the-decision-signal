About

I'm a Data Science and AI leader with 13+ years of experience turning messy, complex business problems into decisions that actually move the needle. I've spent my career at the intersection of advanced analytics, machine learning, and strategic storytelling — working across retail, FMCG, supply chain, and financial services to deliver real, measurable impact.

At Tesco, I currently lead AI-driven transformation of internal audit and risk — deploying cutting-edge ML techniques including Isolation Forests, Generative Adversarial Networks, Network Graph models, and Markov Models to detect anomalies, enhance risk coverage, and dramatically reduce manual effort. I am also architecting an LLM-based conversational intelligence platform that gives the broader Insights team instant access to historical strategic insights.

What drives me is the space between data and decisions — where rigorous science meets real business judgement. I believe the best data scientists are also translators: fluent in both the technical depth of the model and the strategic language of the boardroom. That belief is what led me to create The Decision Signal — a platform for practitioners who want to go beyond the hype.

I'm especially drawn to high-impact roles where I can lead strategic capability development, drive enterprise-scale transformation, and work in environments that value intellectual curiosity, collaboration, and real-world results.
