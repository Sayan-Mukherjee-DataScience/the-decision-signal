Our Commitment to Transparency

The Decision Signal believes in full transparency. This page explains our affiliate relationships so you can make informed decisions when clicking links on this site.

Amazon Associates Programme

The Decision Signal participates in the Amazon Associates Programme, an affiliate advertising programme that allows sites to earn advertising fees by linking to Amazon products.

This means that some links to books and resources in our Library section are affiliate links. If you click one of these links and make a purchase on Amazon, we may earn a small commission — at no additional cost to you. The price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our link or go directly to Amazon.

Which Links Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links on this site appear exclusively in the Library section of the Learn page, where books are listed with a "Buy at Amazon →" button. All such buttons are affiliate links to Amazon.in.

All other links on this site — to articles, research papers, YouTube videos, and external publications — are non-affiliate links. We receive no compensation for those.

How It Affects Our Recommendations

It doesn't. We recommend books because we genuinely believe they are worth reading — not because they generate commission. We would recommend the same books regardless of whether an affiliate programme existed. Our editorial decisions are entirely independent of affiliate relationships.

Free Resources

All resources listed as free in our Library (open-access textbooks, research papers, free PDFs) are genuinely free. There are no affiliate relationships attached to these links.

Questions

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships, please contact us — we're happy to clarify.
