Who We Are

The Decision Signal is an independent publication focused on data science, machine learning, and enterprise AI. We are committed to handling any personal information you share with us responsibly and transparently.

Information We Collect

Newsletter subscriptions: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is used solely to send you the newsletter you signed up for — nothing else.

Contact and coaching enquiries: If you fill in a contact or booking form, we collect the name, email address, and message content you provide. This is used only to respond to your enquiry.

Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand general patterns of site usage — pages visited, time on site, geographic region (country level), and device type. This data is anonymised and aggregated. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Cookies: See our Cookie Policy for full details on what cookies are set and why.

How We Use Your Information

We use information only for the specific purpose it was collected for. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data with third parties under any circumstances.

Third-Party Services

This site uses a small number of third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

  • Formspree — used to process contact and booking form submissions. Subject to Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics — used for anonymised site analytics. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube — embedded video player. YouTube may set cookies when you interact with embedded videos. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for the data practices of these third-party services.

Data Retention

Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form data is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry, and no longer than 12 months.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected] and we will respond within 30 days.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect any information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us.
